Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Information We Collect

When you place an order with Pebble Store, we collect the following information:

  • Full name and contact number

  • Delivery address
  • Order details and preferences

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Process and fulfil your order

  • Send delivery and tracking updates

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is only used internally for order processing and is never shared for marketing purposes.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information. All order data is stored securely and accessible only to authorised personnel.
