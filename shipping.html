Shipping & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Shipping Timeframes

All orders are handmade fresh upon confirmation. Please allow 1-2 business days for preparation before dispatch.

DestinationEstimated Delivery
Tamil Nadu3–5 business days after dispatch
Rest of India5–7 business days after dispatch

Delivery times are estimates and may vary depending on the courier and your location. Remote areas may take longer.

Shipping Partners

We ship via trusted courier partners including ST Courier, DTDC, Professional Courier, and India Post depending on your location.

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges, if applicable, will be communicated to you before order confirmation. We strive to keep shipping costs minimal.

Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking ID via WhatsApp. You can track your order at pebblestore.com/track.

Cancellation Policy

⚠️ No cancellations are accepted once an order is placed and confirmed.
Since all our products are handmade to order, production begins immediately upon confirmation. We are unable to cancel orders after this point. Please review your order carefully before confirming.

For shipping-related queries, please contact us via WhatsApp at +91 89254 01892.
